Software Development

Our team at Hudson Linux has expertise in software for Linux, Android, Windows and iOS. We have particular expertise in writing software that integrates Bluetooth connectivity. Comfortable working in C++, Python, C, Kotlin, C#, and more. Here is a gallery of some software projects we've completed:

  • Bluetooth Meatball, an interesting video game prototype. Uses the cellphone accelerometer + bluetooth to make an interesting 2 player game.
